Certification Pathways

Three levels of certification from practitioner to master. Build expertise progressively.

CSP Level 1

Certified SuperCycle Practitioner

Investment $500-$800

Foundation certification for analysts and associates entering strategic intelligence.

Curriculum:

  • Control Factors (Dominion, Contingency, Influence)
  • STEEPLED Framework
  • LIST Basics & Action Graph
  • Missionary/Mercenary Mindsets

Outcome

Contribute data to The LIST and participate in rituals

CRS Level 2

Certified Role Specialist

Investment $1,500-$2,000

Specialize in one phase: Discovery, Optimality, or Simulation.

Specialization Tracks:

  • CRS-Discovery (Landscape Architect)
  • CRS-Optimality (Superiority Strategist)
  • CRS-Simulation (Simulation Commander)

Outcome

Independently own one SuperCycle phase and operate the corresponding app

CSM Level 3

Certified SuperCycle Master

Investment $3,000+

Install and orchestrate the SuperCycle across entire organizations.

Prerequisites:

  • Level 1: CSP Certification
  • Level 2: One CRS Specialization

Outcome

Implement SuperCycle enterprise-wide and lead organizational transformation

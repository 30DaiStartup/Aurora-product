# SuperCycle Operating System

*Codifying the Next Generation of Strategic Intelligence*

---

## Overview

The SuperCycle is a business operating system (BOS) that replaces static, annual "strategic planning" with a dynamic, continuous, AI-enabled engine for decision-making.

**The Three Phases:**
1. **Discovery** - Revealing truth about the landscape
2. **Optimality** - Making strategic choices
3. **Simulation** - Testing the strategy

---

## The SuperCycle Manifesto

*Challenging "Static Strategy" as Agile challenged Waterfall*

We are uncovering better ways to navigate **Stochasm** and **Under-certainty** by doing it and helping others do it. Through this work, we have come to value:

| We Value More | Over |
|---------------|------|
| **Dynamic Recalibration** | Annual Strategic Planning |
| **Investment-Grade Conjecture** | Executive Gut Instinct |
| **Actionable Esteem** | Analysis Paralysis |
| **Missionary Truth & Mercenary Action** | Blind idealism or soulless execution |

> Strategy is not a document; it is a continuous loop of **Discovery, Optimality, and Simulation**.

## The Technology Ecosystem (The "Office Suite")

### Aurora Discovery (The Sensor)

**Function**: AI agents continuously scrape macro-data (STEEPLED)

**Output**: Dynamic LIST that alerts when "Truth" changes
- Example: "Competitor X just lowered prices"
- Example: "New Tariff Y enacted"

### Aurora Optimality (The Decider)

**Function**: Digital Choice Cascade board

**Input**: Feeds from Discovery

**Action**: Strategist toggles choices (Winning Aspiration, Where to Play). If Discovery feed turns red, Strategist must acknowledge risk or change choice.

**Output**: IGC (versioned and tracked)

### Aurora Simulation (The Wargame)

**Function**: Agent-based modeling where AI personas (representing competitors/regulators) react to your IGC

**Output**: Actionable Esteem (probability scores of success)

## The Rituals

### Proactive Rituals (The Heartbeat)

#### 1. The Morning "LIST" Check (Daily)

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Purpose | Validate Contingency Factors; ensure Discovery App hasn't detected boundary shift |
| Timebox | 15 Minutes |
| Owner | Landscape Architect |
| Attendees | SuperCycle Master, Superiority Strategist |
| Output | Green/Amber/Red status on macro-environment |

**The Ritual:**
1. Review AI-generated LIST dashboard
2. **Missionary Check**: New signals of industry change or non-consumption opportunities?
3. If "Red" → trigger Stochasm Swarm

#### 2. The Optimality Orbit (Bi-Weekly)

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Purpose | Review Dominion Factors; ensure Choice Cascade alignment |
| Timebox | 60 Minutes |
| Owner | Superiority Strategist |
| Attendees | Full Strategy Squad |
| Output | Updated IGC |

**The Ritual:**
1. **FITU Review**: Does Value Proposition still hold against latest competitor data?
2. **Superiority Check**: Are Drivers degrading into Satisfiers? Over-investing in unrewarded features?
3. **Mercenary Decision**: Explicitly "Select and Sacrifice" options based on last two weeks' data

#### 3. The Simulation Pressure Test (Quarterly)

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| Purpose | Harden Influence Factors via wargaming |
| Timebox | 4 Hours (Half Day) |
| Owner | Simulation Commander |
| Output | Validated Actionable Esteem |

**The Ritual:**
1. Run "Simulation App" using updated IGC against AI personas
2. **Guile & Empathy**: Team role-plays the "Enemy"
3. **Destruction**: Goal is to break current strategy
4. If strategy survives → ratified

### Reactive Rituals (The Immune Response)

#### 4. The Stochasm Swarm (Trigger: Contingency Shift)

**Trigger**: AI/Landscape Architect detects high-certainty, high-impact STEEPLED change

> "The Mercenary understands that no plan will survive engagement with the enemy."

**The Ritual:**
1. **Stop the Line**: Pause tactical execution related to affected factor
2. **Gap Analysis**: Identify new Stochasm (gap between assumed and actual reality)
3. **Recalibration**: Adjust Dominion Factors to cope with new Contingency
4. **Output**: Immediate "Patch" to Strategy

#### 5. The Influence Intercept (Trigger: Stakeholder/Competitor Move)

**Trigger**: Key stakeholder behaves unexpectedly; Action Graph shows spike in "Imminence"

**The Ritual:**
1. **Simulation Run**: Input new event into Simulation App
2. **Prediction**: App predicts "Next Best Alternative" for stakeholder
3. **Counter-Move**: Develop counter-move to regain Control
4. **Output**: Tactical directive to sales/marketing/product teams

## The Three Core Artifacts

### Artifact 1: The LIST (Output of Discovery)

**Purpose**: Define boundaries of macro-environment and the Stochasm

**Owner**: Landscape Architect

**Structure:**
1. **Landscape (L)**: Scope definition (TAM/SAM), linguistic boundaries
2. **Issues (I)**: Current friction, unmet needs, jobs to be done
3. **Stakeholders (S)**: Key actors with Missionary/Mercenary mindset profiles
4. **Trends (T)**: STEEPLED dashboard with Green/Amber/Red status

### Artifact 2: The Investment Grade Conjecture (IGC) (Output of Optimality)

**Purpose**: Document "Select and Sacrifice" choices (Dominion Factors)

**Owner**: Superiority Strategist

**Structure:**
1. **Choice Cascade**:
   - Winning Aspiration
   - Where to Play
   - How to Win
   - Capabilities
   - Management Systems

2. **FITU Statement**:
   - **F**OR: Target Market/Persona
   - **I**T'S: The Offer
   - **T**HAT: Delivers distinct benefit
   - **U**NLIKE: Next best alternatives

3. **Superiority Analysis Matrix**:
   - Satisfiers (minimum table stakes - don't over-invest)
   - Drivers (competitive differentiators - temporary advantage)
   - Disruptors (new performance dimensions - Gamma/Delta moves)

4. **Stochasm Questions**: Specific unknowns that must be answered to reduce risk

### Artifact 3: Actionable Esteem (Output of Simulation)

**Purpose**: Convert "story about the future" into scientifically validated probability

**Owner**: Simulation Commander

**Structure:**
1. **Action Graph Coordinates**:
   - Impact (X): How significant?
   - Imminence (Y): How soon?
   - Certainty (Z): Confidence level (0-100%)

2. **Influence Factors (Simulation Results)**:
   - Home Team Move: Proposed IGC
   - Competition Team Counter-Move: Imitate, Leapfrog, or Surrender
   - Stakeholder Reaction: Support or block?

3. **Probability Score**:
   - Success Probability (e.g., "78% Chance of achieving Winning Aspiration")
   - Fragility Score: Where strategy most likely to break

4. **The "Martian" Protocol**:
   - Scientific Explanation: Removing "magic" and "luck"
   - Step-by-step logic chain of WHY this will work

## The Role of the SuperCycle Master

The SuperCycle Master is the guardian of the **Action Graph**:

- Monitors dashboard where Impact, Imminence, and Certainty are visualized
- If a variable moves past threshold (e.g., Certainty drops below 60%), triggers appropriate reactive ritual
- Ensures Missionary and Mercenary mindsets are balanced during meetings
- Prevents team from getting paralyzed by the infinite (Missionary) or ignoring long-term consequences for quick wins (Mercenary)
